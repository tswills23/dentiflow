# Implant Consultation

**Category:** Restorative / Oral Surgery

## What It Is
An evaluation to determine if a patient is a good candidate for a dental implant — a titanium post surgically placed in the jawbone that acts as an artificial tooth root. The consult typically includes X-rays or a 3D scan and a discussion of the treatment plan, timeline, and cost.

## Common Aliases
- Implant consultation
- Dental implant consult
- Implant evaluation
- Tooth replacement consult
- Missing tooth consultation
- Implant assessment

## Typical Duration
30-60 minutes for the consultation. The full implant process (placement + healing + crown) spans 3-6 months.

## Insurance Note
Implant coverage varies widely. Some plans cover a portion, many do not, and some cover the crown but not the implant itself. Tell patients: "Implant coverage varies by plan. Our team will verify your benefits and go over all your financial options, including payment plans if needed."

## Key Patient Questions

**Q: How much do implants cost?**
A: "Pricing depends on your specific needs and whether insurance covers any portion. The consultation is the best way to get a clear picture of cost — our team will go over everything with you."

**Q: How long does the whole process take?**
A: "The full process typically takes a few months from start to finish. The doctor will walk you through the timeline during your consultation."

**Q: Does it hurt?**
A: "The procedure is done with anesthesia so you're comfortable throughout. Most patients say the recovery is easier than they expected."

**Q: Am I a candidate for an implant?**
A: Do NOT assess candidacy. Say: "That's exactly what the consultation is for! The doctor will evaluate everything and let you know the best options for you."

**Q: I've been missing a tooth for a long time — is it too late?**
A: "It's never too late to explore your options. The consultation will give the doctor a chance to evaluate and discuss what's possible."

## Booking Guidance
- Always book a consultation first — never book implant surgery directly
- Appointment type: "Implant Consultation" or "Implant Consult"
- If the patient mentions cost concerns, mention that the practice can discuss payment plans during the consult

## Tone Guidance
Encouraging and informative. Implants represent a significant investment and decision. Be positive about the life-changing results without overselling. Make the consultation feel low-pressure — it's just an evaluation.
