# Teeth Whitening

**Category:** Cosmetic

## What It Is
A cosmetic procedure that lightens the shade of your teeth using a professional-grade bleaching agent. Results are significantly more effective and longer-lasting than over-the-counter products. Available as in-office treatment (immediate results) or take-home custom trays.

## Common Aliases
- Teeth bleaching
- Tooth whitening
- Professional whitening
- Zoom whitening
- In-office whitening
- Whitening trays
- Cosmetic whitening
- Smile brightening

## Typical Duration
In-office: 60-90 minutes (single visit). Take-home trays: 1-2 weeks of daily use, with a short visit for impressions/fitting.

## Insurance Note
Whitening is almost never covered by dental insurance as it is considered a cosmetic procedure. Tell patients: "Whitening is a cosmetic service, so it's typically not covered by insurance. Our team can go over pricing and any specials we're currently running."

## Key Patient Questions

**Q: How much does whitening cost?**
A: "Pricing depends on the type of whitening that's best for you. We can go over options and pricing when you come in — there may be specials available too."

**Q: How white will my teeth get?**
A: "Results vary from person to person. The doctor can give you a realistic idea of what to expect during your visit."

**Q: Is it safe? Will it damage my teeth?**
A: "Professional whitening is safe and supervised by our dental team. It's very different from some over-the-counter products. The doctor can answer any specific concerns."

**Q: How long do results last?**
A: "That depends on a few factors like diet and habits. The team can give you tips on maintaining your results."

**Q: I have sensitive teeth — can I still whiten?**
A: "That's a great question to discuss with the doctor. There are options for patients with sensitivity, and they'll recommend what's best for you."

## Booking Guidance
- Appointment type: "Whitening Consultation" or "Cosmetic Consultation"
- If the practice offers whitening specials or packages, mention them
- Some practices include whitening with new patient exams — check practice_config

## Tone Guidance
Fun and aspirational. Whitening is an exciting, confidence-boosting service. Keep the energy positive and focus on the results. This isn't a medical conversation — it's about feeling great about their smile.
