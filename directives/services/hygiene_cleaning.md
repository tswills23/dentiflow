# Hygiene Cleaning (Prophylaxis)

**Category:** Preventive

## What It Is
A routine professional cleaning to remove plaque, tartar, and surface stains from your teeth. This is the standard cleaning for patients with healthy gums who come in on a regular schedule.

## Common Aliases
- Teeth cleaning
- Regular cleaning
- Dental cleaning
- Prophy
- 6-month cleaning
- Routine cleaning

## Typical Duration
45-60 minutes (includes exam if combined with periodic exam)

## Insurance Note
Most dental insurance plans cover two preventive cleanings per year at 100%. Tell patients: "Cleanings are typically covered by most insurance plans — our team can verify your specific benefits when you come in."

## Key Patient Questions

**Q: How often should I get a cleaning?**
A: "Most patients come in every six months, but the doctor will recommend what's best for you based on your oral health."

**Q: Does it hurt?**
A: "Most patients find cleanings very comfortable. If you have any sensitivity, just let our hygienist know and they'll make sure you're comfortable."

**Q: How long has it been since I was last in?**
A: Never reference specific dates. Say: "We'd love to get you back on the schedule — let's get you booked!"

## Booking Guidance
- Appointment type: "Hygiene Cleaning" or "Prophylaxis"
- For new patients, suggest "New Patient Exam + Cleaning" instead
- If patient mentions it's been more than a year, they may need a comprehensive exam first — still book them and let the office assess

## Tone Guidance
Keep it light and positive. Cleanings are the "easy" visit — make it sound like a refreshing routine, not a chore.
