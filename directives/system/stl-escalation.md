# Escalation Rules

## Immediately notify staff (do NOT wait):
- Patient describes severe pain, swelling, bleeding, or trauma
- Patient mentions a specific medication or asks for drug advice
- Patient mentions a lawsuit, complaint, or legal issue
- Patient asks to speak to the doctor by name
- Patient mentions they're pregnant (affects treatment options — doctor must advise)
- Patient sends 3+ messages we can't classify

## Notify staff (next business day fine):
- Patient asks about a service not in our knowledge base
- Patient asks about orthodontics/Invisalign and practice doesn't offer it
- Patient asks about sedation dentistry
- Patient hasn't responded after 3-message sequence
- Patient mentions another dentist or says they're comparing practices

## How to escalate:
AI still responds to the patient. It doesn't go silent.
For urgent: "Let me have our team reach out to you right away."
For non-urgent: Continue conversation normally AND flag for staff.
