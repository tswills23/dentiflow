## Trending Topics

- **AI dental receptionists exploding in 2026** (source: websearch) -- Dentina.ai, Arini, Viva AI, HeyGent, Savvy Agents all competing. AI-booked appointments hitting 90% show rate. This is the competitive landscape Dentiflow is entering.
- **PPO fee negotiation as growth strategy** (source: websearch, dentaleconomics) -- Dentists write off ~40% in PPO reimbursements ($4K of every $10K). Most never renegotiate. 90-120 day process but high ROI.
- **Review velocity as patient acquisition lever** (source: websearch) -- 93% of patients read reviews before choosing. 84% trust them as much as friend recommendations. Multi-channel reputation strategy emerging (beyond Google-only).
- **13% revenue decline pattern** (source: dentaleconomics) -- Dental Economics published on why practices fail to grow and how to reverse a 13% decline. Systems-level thinking, not more marketing.
- **Scheduling structure over fill** (source: websearch, mgeonline) -- MGE published on "common scheduling issues that quietly reduce revenue." High-production procedures during peak energy, buffer time for same-day treatment. Benchmark: $1,500/day per hygienist.

## Content Gaps (What No One Is Posting About)

- **Review-to-referral pipeline**: Everyone talks about getting reviews. Nobody connects review collection to referral automation as one system.
- **No-show recovery as revenue system**: Posts frame no-shows as a reminder problem. Nobody frames it as a multi-step recovery system with rebooking sequences.
- **PPO math for practice owners**: Tons of content about "negotiate your fees" but almost none showing the actual math of what 5-10% fee increases compound to across a full patient panel.
- **Speed-to-lead data in dental specifically**: Harvard speed-to-lead data gets cited generically. Nobody is running actual tests in dental practices and sharing results.
- **AI receptionist comparison from a builder's perspective**: Lots of vendor marketing. Zero founder-level content explaining what these systems actually do under the hood.
