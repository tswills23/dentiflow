# Source Doc 19: JP Roadmap + Market Positioning

## Strategic Context

Dentiflow is positioned as a complete AI revenue system for PPO dental practices. Not a point solution. Not a marketing agency. A system that plugs revenue leaks across the entire patient lifecycle.

The market is full of single-service vendors: SEO agencies, PPC managers, recall postcard companies, phone systems. Each solves one piece. None of them solve the system.

## The Bundle Advantage

Single services are commodities. Bundled systems create lock-in and compound results.

When recall recovery runs alongside speed-to-lead, patients who respond to recall texts get fast follow-up. When review automation runs alongside recall, reactivated patients who have a great visit become review sources. When no-show recovery runs alongside booking, confirmed patients show up and generate production.

Each module amplifies the others. This is the core argument for the full system.

## Why PPO Dental

- PPO practices are insurance-dependent, volume-driven, and operationally complex.
- They have the highest revenue leakage because they process the most patients with the most moving parts.
- They're underserved by tech. Most dental software is either overly complex (PMS systems) or too simple (postcard recall).
- PPO practices have predictable unit economics: known production per visit, known reimbursement rates, calculable ROI.
- The total addressable market is large: ~120,000 dental practices in the US, majority are PPO-based.

## Competitive Landscape

- **Weave / RevenueWell / Dental Intelligence:** All-in-one platforms but expensive, complex, require long onboarding. Not AI-native.
- **Marketing agencies:** Solve demand generation but nothing downstream. Leads come in, nobody follows up.
- **Postcard recall companies:** Analog, low response rates (1-3%), no digital follow-up loop.
- **AI voice companies (Retell, Vapi):** Solve phone answering but nothing else. No recall, no reviews, no no-show recovery.

Dentiflow's edge: AI-native from day one, modular (practices can start with one module and add), purpose-built for PPO practices, and priced for mid-market (not enterprise).

## The Offer Architecture

Inspired by Acquisition.com methodology:

1. **Anchor offer:** SMS Recall Recovery + Speed-to-Lead. Immediate ROI. Pays for itself in month 1.
2. **Expansion:** Add Reviews + Referrals and No-Show Recovery. Compounds the results.
3. **Premium upsell:** AI Voice Receptionist. The complete hands-off front desk.
4. **Dashboard:** White-label analytics showing all module performance. The practice owner's command center.

The guarantee structure: performance-based. If the system doesn't recover X in the first 90 days, you don't pay. Risk reversal makes the sale frictionless.
