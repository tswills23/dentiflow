# REVENUE CAPTURE -- Complete Breakdown

## 1. Core Actions

### A. Clear Identification of Unscheduled Treatment

**What must happen:**
- Every exam results in treatment scheduled or explicitly marked unscheduled

**Why this is required:**
You cannot recover what you do not label. Unscheduled treatment must be visible.

**What breaks if missing:**
- Treatment disappears into charts
- No follow-up occurs
- Ownership confusion

### B. Immediate Post-Exam Framing

**What must happen:**
- Explain what is needed
- Explain why timing matters
- Define the next step

**Why this is required:**
Urgency is highest in the chair. It decays rapidly after the visit.

**What breaks if missing:**
- Treatment feels optional
- Procrastination

### C. Financial Clarity

**What must happen:**
- Cost range explained
- Insurance expectation clarified
- Payment options outlined

**Why this is required:**
Uncertainty creates perceived risk, which causes delay.

**What breaks if missing:**
- "Need to check insurance"
- "Need to talk to spouse"

### D. Structured Follow-Up for Unscheduled Treatment

**What must happen:**
- Patient entered into follow-up sequence
- Outreach planned intentionally

**Why this is required:**
Most patients say "not now," not "no."

**What breaks if missing:**
- One weak follow-up
- Staff discomfort calling later

### E. Re-Entry Points

**What must happen:**
- Revisit treatment during recall or hygiene

**Why this is required:**
Readiness changes over time.

**What breaks if missing:**
- Deferred patients lost permanently

---

## 2. Ownership (Human vs System)

**System-owned:**
- Flag unscheduled treatment
- Follow-up cadence

**Human-owned:**
- Diagnosis and framing
- Financial explanation
- Live re-engagement

**Why this split is required:**
Doctors diagnose, systems remember, humans re-engage.

---

## 3. Failure Modes

- "They'll Call When Ready": Passive surrender.
- No Follow-Up Ownership: Assumptions replace action.
- One Awkward Call: Discomfort kills consistency.
- No Time-Based Logic: Old and new treatment treated the same.

---

## 4. Recovery Logic

- Didn't schedule → Enter follow-up sequence
- No response → Multi-touch over time
- Declined → Revisit at hygiene
- Cost concern → Reframe later
- Fear → Education-based follow-up

**Why this is required:**
Revenue capture is non-linear.

---

## 5. Outputs

Revenue Capture must output:
- Treatment scheduled
- Treatment completed
- Explicitly declined (with reason)
- Deferred with re-engagement plan

Anything else is leakage.
